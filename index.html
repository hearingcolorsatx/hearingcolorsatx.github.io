Hearing Colors

Independent, artist-owned catalog of art-forward music.

Hearing Colors LLC is an independent, artist-owned record label founded by Tim Delaney. Built on direct ownership and disciplined craft, the company produces music with both emotional weight and practical application.

The current catalog moves between two distinct voices—intimate, harmony-driven songwriting and concept-forward, rhythm-based production—each created with artistic integrity and narrative awareness.

Hearing Colors possesses a simple advantage: clarity. Clear ownership. Clear intent. Clean execution. Music that connects when heard—and holds when placed.

Music should serve the story, not distract from it.
