Hearing Colors

Art-forward music for picture, story and sync.

Independent, artist-owned catalog built for film, advertising, and modern media.

Hearing Colors LLC is an artist-owned record label and sync-focused creative studio founded in 2019, with roots tracing back to 2006. Conceived during founder Tim Delaney’s transition from military service into music production, the company was built on a simple principle: quality music, owned by the artist, delivered through a clear and flexible licensing structure.

Operating as a hybrid label and licensing boutique, Hearing Colors develops emotionally resonant recordings while maintaining full rights ownership and direct clearance capability. The catalog spans intimate, harmony-driven songwriting under Tim Delaney and experimental, rhythm-forward production under neutron squared—two distinct creative identities united by intentional craft, narrative awareness, and sync viability.

Hearing Colors exists to simplify what has become an increasingly fragmented licensing ecosystem. With a commitment to transparency, artistic integrity, and professional discipline, the company bridges creative expression and practical utility—producing music designed not only to be heard, but to work.

Music should serve the story, not distract from it.
